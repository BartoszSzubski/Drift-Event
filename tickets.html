Drift Eevent 2023

June 24th-25th 2023 Drift Arena Poznań, Poland

Prepare for an adrenaline-pumping spectacle as drift enthusiasts from all over converge in Poznań, Poland for an exhilarating drift event. With the roaring engines and screeching tires, professional drivers will demonstrate their exceptional skills, executing precision drifts and heart-stopping maneuvers that defy gravity. Spectators will be captivated by the high-speed action, feeling the rush of excitement as they witness the mastery of control and the artistry of drifting in this thrilling event.

Get ready for an electrifying drift event in Poznań, Poland, where the thrill of high-speed drifting awaits. Experience the excitement firsthand with tickets priced at 99 PLN per person. Students can avail themselves of a discounted rate of 79 PLN, making it an affordable and adrenaline-packed event for all motorsport enthusiasts.
